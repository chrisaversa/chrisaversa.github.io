About me

My name is Chris Aversa and I am a 26 year old automotive engineer. I graduated from the University of Windsor with a bachelors in applied science (Mechanical Engineering) in 2014. After completing my undergraduate degree, I went on to pursue a master's degree in automotive engineering. Throughout my research I published 6 papers on the topic of internal combustion engine emissions. I currently reside in southern California.
